CAD & 3D PRINTING

At the core of modern product development and prototyping, CAD (Computer-Aided Design) and 3D Printing stand as pivotal tools for new product development, enabling us to transform complex ideas into precise digital models and physical prototypes.

Our services cater to both professionals and hobbyists, supporting a wide range of projects. We have expertise and a proven track record in developing industrial parts/enclosures, replicating broken/worn parts at home/work, building custom parts for RC enthusiasts, or crafting unique accessories for cosplay.

We bring your concepts to life with the highest level of accuracy and creativity, making us a trusted partner for all your CAD and 3D printing needs.

CAD (Computer-Aided Design)

CAD allows us to design, refine, and visualize projects in stunning detail before moving to production. Our CAD expertise spans various industries, from engineering and industrial design to custom accessories and RC part development. We work closely with clients to ensure each design meets their functional and aesthetic requirements, allowing us to deliver exceptional results every time.

Our CAD services include:

  • Detailed engineering drawings, furniture design and 3D modeling for new products and custom parts

  • Specialized designs for industrial enclosures, cosplay weapons and accessories, and creative prototypes

  • Custom designs for RC builders, car accessories, desk accessories, cable management, and more

3D PRINTING (FDM)

Our 3D Printing service takes your CAD designs from digital models to tangible products, layer by layer. With our advanced 3D printing capabilities, we provide fast, efficient, and cost-effective solutions for everything from industrial parts to custom cosplay accessories. Whether you need a prototype, a fully functional part, or a decorative piece, our 3D printing services allow you to explore endless possibilities.

With 3D Printing, we offer:

  • 3D Printing for Hobbyists and Professionals

  • Custom RC parts

  • Wall Mount designs and custome Crocs Charms

  • Industrial enclosures and engineering prototypes for new product development

  • Personalized cosplay weapons, comic accessories, and Comicon collectibles

  • Everyday desk accessories, pen holders, and cable management solutions

  • Support for both small-batch production and one-of-a-kind items
